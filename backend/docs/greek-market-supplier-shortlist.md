# Greek-Market Supplier Shortlist

This is a starter list of companies active in Greece whose public sites already expose useful data for a Foodbase-style supplier graph.

It is not an exhaustive market map. It is the first research set worth loading into the system and prioritizing for outreach.

## Tier 1: Integrated Food Producers And Private-Label Targets

### Vamvalis Foods

- Why it matters: strong end-to-end candidate from sourcing through packaging and export.
- Publicly listed coverage: sourcing, R&D, manufacturing, packaging, warehousing, logistics and shipping.
- Publicly listed offerings: nuts, seeds, dried fruits, spices, binding ingredients, bars and bites.
- Publicly listed capabilities: custom product design, large packaging range, pallet-scale entry, shipping to 30+ countries.
- Publicly listed certifications: IFS, SMETA, AIB, ISO 22000.
- Source: https://www.vamvalisfoods.com/our-process/
- Outreach focus: MOQ by format, exact private-label categories, annual capacity by line, incoterms, Greece-first distribution terms.

### MYST

- Why it matters: branded and B2B Greek food producer with broad category coverage and explicit HORECA footprint.
- Publicly listed coverage: product categories, HORECA, partner sales, Greek and Swedish office/warehouse presence.
- Publicly listed offerings: aromatic herbs, olive oil, olives and pickles, tea, honey and bee pollen, salt, spices, seasonings, shots and drinks.
- Publicly listed capabilities: B2B partnership, HORECA catalog, organic positioning, multi-market sales contact structure.
- Publicly listed certifications: organic marks shown on site; exact operational certification set should be confirmed in outreach.
- Source: https://www.myst.gr/contact/
- Outreach focus: private-label availability, packaging formats, MOQ, export terms, facility certifications, lead times.

### Paltsidis Hellenic Gastronomy

- Why it matters: clear private-label manufacturer across chilled and shelf-stable Mediterranean food categories.
- Publicly listed coverage: retail, HORECA, private label.
- Publicly listed offerings: vegan products, dips, salads, refrigerated sauces, shelf-stable sauces, mustard and ketchup, Greek dessert, seafood, tarama paste, antipasti, field products.
- Publicly listed capabilities: personalized recipes, packaging options, product design and development support.
- Publicly listed certifications: site emphasizes global food-safety standards; exact certificates should be collected directly.
- Source: https://www.paltsidis.gr/en/private-label/
- Outreach focus: exact certifications, MOQ, chilled versus ambient capabilities, export coverage, available packaging formats.

### Novafood

- Why it matters: highly structured sweetener and jam manufacturer with explicit private-label support.
- Publicly listed coverage: sweetener production, jams, private label, customized products.
- Publicly listed offerings: classic sweeteners, stevia products, jams.
- Publicly listed capabilities: tailor-made production, private label.
- Publicly listed certifications: HACCP, ISO 22000:2005, BRC, FDA Registered.
- Source: https://novafood.gr/
- Outreach focus: packaging formats, MOQ by product family, export markets, formulation constraints, lead times.

### Gusto Dairy

- Why it matters: dairy and cheese producer with explicit private-label focus in Greece and abroad.
- Publicly listed coverage: dairy manufacturing, private label, vegan product line.
- Publicly listed offerings: feta PDO, white cheese crumbles, manouri PDO, semi-hard cheese, gouda, mozzarella, white and yellow cheese alternatives, vegan mozzarella.
- Publicly listed capabilities: private label for domestic and international markets.
- Publicly listed certifications: product documents mention certification logos and technical specifications, but the exact full plant-level certification set should be confirmed.
- Source: https://www.gustodairy.gr/private-label/?lang=en
- Outreach focus: private-label packaging formats, refrigerated logistics setup, MOQ, export documentation, audit pack.

### DAMA

- Why it matters: strong produce-side candidate tied to Greek supermarket private label and field-to-shelf traceability.
- Publicly listed coverage: fruits, vegetables, private label, traceability, supermarket supply.
- Publicly listed offerings: fresh produce categories, especially fruit and vegetables.
- Publicly listed capabilities: private label for large supermarket chains, certified production, traceability from field to shelf.
- Publicly listed certifications: site claims certified production; exact names should be collected directly.
- Source: https://www.dama.com.gr/en/private-label/
- Outreach focus: crop categories by season, packing formats, cold-chain coverage, regional availability, certifications by facility.

### Nosgea

- Why it matters: food manufacturer oriented to chefs, foodservice, and custom culinary products.
- Publicly listed coverage: product development, foodservice, custom products.
- Publicly listed offerings: sauces, broths, blends, culinary ingredients and meal-prep oriented products.
- Publicly listed capabilities: industrial and foodservice orientation, branded product families, likely useful for sauces and prepared foods.
- Publicly listed certifications: not clearly exposed on the public pages reviewed.
- Source: https://nosgea.gr/
- Outreach focus: exact private-label program, production certifications, MOQ, packaging types, export readiness.

## Tier 2: Packaging Partners

### VEKIOS / Ranco Pak

- Why it matters: Greek packaging supplier already structured around food-contact packaging workflows.
- Publicly listed coverage: design, prototyping, mold construction, thermoformed packaging production.
- Publicly listed offerings: bowls, clamshells, lids and other thermoformed plastic food containers.
- Publicly listed capabilities: custom mold creation, food packaging production, recycled PET product line.
- Publicly listed certifications: ISO 9001:2015, ISO 22000:2018.
- Source: https://www.vekios.gr/en/
- Outreach focus: food category fit, MOQs for custom molds, lead times, recyclable-material options, labeling and decoration support.

### VIS

- Publicly listed coverage: carton boxes, industrial and agricultural packaging.
- Publicly listed offerings: 3-layer and 5-layer cartons, printed packaging, bag-in-box lines, agricultural packaging.
- Publicly listed capabilities: custom dimensions, multiple flute types, up to 4-color printing.
- Publicly listed certifications: FSSC 22000 appears on product pages; confirm exact scope during outreach.

### MACPAC

- Why it matters: practical packaging partner for produce and fresh-food categories.
- Publicly listed coverage: food-approved recyclable PET packaging.
- Publicly listed offerings: clamshell punnets and related produce packaging.
- Publicly listed capabilities: packaging compatible with manual and automatic punnet-closing machines.
- Publicly listed certifications: confirm in outreach.

## Tier 3: Cold Chain, Warehousing, And Distribution

### EXELIXIS

- Why it matters: strong 3PL candidate focused on food and beverage.
- Publicly listed coverage: receiving, stock control, storage, order management, picking, loading, and distribution.
- Publicly listed offerings: 3PL services, micro-picking, distribution, routing, real-time tracking.
- Publicly listed capabilities: traceability, WMS, FEFO rotation, multi-temperature fleet, 6,000 to 7,000+ delivery points.
- Publicly listed certifications: IFS Logistics, ISO 9000, ISO 22000.
- Source: https://exelixis-horeca.gr/en/ypiresies/logistics-3pl/
- Outreach focus: temperature coverage by lane, SLA terms, regions served, API/EDI support, onboarding process.

### Arcafroz

- Why it matters: useful for chilled, frozen, and specialty storage for agricultural and dairy products.
- Publicly listed coverage: refrigerated and frozen storage, modified-atmosphere storage, dairy ripening, traceability-aware stock administration.
- Publicly listed offerings: 3PL cold storage and delivery services.
- Publicly listed capabilities: ULO storage for apples and pears, high humidity cheese storage, beehive low-temperature storage, online stock visibility.
- Publicly listed certifications: confirm in outreach.
- Source: https://www.arcafroz.gr/en/logistics-2/
- Outreach focus: service geography, minimum storage volumes, transport fleet setup, ERP connectivity.

### Lomvardos Cold Stores

- Why it matters: useful regional cold-storage and logistics node, especially if Foodbase expands into central Greece.
- Publicly listed coverage: logistics services and cold-storage operation in Volos industrial zone.
- Publicly listed offerings: refrigerated logistics and storage.
- Publicly listed capabilities: confirm through direct outreach; the public site is sparse.
- Publicly listed certifications: not clearly exposed on the public pages reviewed.
- Source: https://www.lomvardos.gr/default.aspx?lang=en
- Outreach focus: temperature zones, service radius, warehouse specs, handling standards, commercial terms.
