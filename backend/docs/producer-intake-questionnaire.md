# Producer Intake Questionnaire

Use this for email outreach, onboarding calls, or a supplier self-serve form.

## Company Basics

- Legal company name
- Display brand name
- VAT number / company registration number
- Website
- Headquarters address
- Main operating facilities
- Primary contact name, role, email, and phone
- Languages supported for business communication

## Market Presence

- Do you currently sell in Greece?
- Which regions of Greece do you serve?
- Do you export outside Greece? If yes, where?
- Which channels do you serve?
  retail, HORECA, wholesale, distributors, private label, industrial buyers

## Supply-Chain Coverage

- Which stages do you cover?
  ingredient sourcing, farming, primary processing, product development, manufacturing, private label, packaging, warehousing, cold chain, distribution, export shipping
- Which stages are in-house and which are outsourced?

## Product and Service Offerings

- Core product categories
- Specific products or SKUs
- Raw ingredients you can supply in bulk
- Finished products you manufacture
- Packaging formats you support
- Logistics or storage services you provide
- Whether you support custom formulation or recipe development

## Commercial Terms

- Minimum order quantity by product or format
- Typical lead times
- Sample policy
- Private label availability
- White label availability
- Pricing model
  quote per unit, per pallet, per container, custom
- Preferred contract length

## Operations and Facilities

- Facility locations
- Production capacity
- Storage capacity
- Temperature zones supported
  ambient, chilled, frozen
- Traceability method
- ERP/WMS/EDI support
- Incoterms or shipping terms supported

## Quality and Compliance

- Food-safety certifications
- Product-specific certifications
  organic, vegan, halal, kosher, gluten-free, PDO/PGI
- Audit history
- Recall procedure
- Allergens handled on site

## Packaging and Labeling

- Packaging materials available
- Packaging design support
- Labeling languages supported
- Barcode / GS1 support
- Shelf-life support and stability data

## Logistics and Shipping

- Own fleet or third-party logistics
- Areas served
- Cold-chain support
- Last-mile distribution support
- Export documentation support
- Real-time tracking availability

## Documents To Request

- Product catalog
- Certification certificates
- Technical data sheets
- Price list
- MOQ and lead-time sheet
- Sample packaging specs
- Company presentation

## What Must Be Captured Even If The Supplier Is Busy

If the supplier only gives partial information, do not end the intake without:

1. contact details
2. stage coverage
3. product categories
4. certifications
5. MOQ
6. lead times
7. packaging formats
8. delivery coverage
